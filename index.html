About Us

Guangdong Zhongke Juneng New Materials Technology Co., Ltd. specializes in the R&D and production of modified PC alloy, modified nylon alloy, modified ABS alloy, modified PBT alloy and other modified alloy materials. The company has a complete scientific quality management system, modern R&D and production base and professional testing center. We are committed to providing stable, high-performance engineering plastic solutions for customers in automotive, electronics, home appliances and other industries.

PC+PBT Alloy

Combines chemical resistance, heat resistance and molding performance of PBT with toughness, dimensional stability and weather resistance of PC.

Excellent stress cracking resistance to gasoline, lubricants, oils and alcohols.

Applications: Automotive parts, bumpers, housings, injection panels, mobile shells.

< img src="pc-abs.jpg" alt="PC+ABS Alloy">

PC+ABS Alloy

Combines good processability of ABS and high mechanical & thermal stability of PC with excellent flowability.

Applications: Auto parts, mobile casings, instruments, optical parts, large thin-wall products.

< img src="pc-asa.jpg" alt="PC+ASA Alloy">

PC+ASA Alloy

Heat resistance and high toughness from PC; weather resistance, UV resistance and good processability from ASA.

Applications: Outdoor products, home appliances, auto sunroofs, antenna boxes, camera equipment.

< img src="pc-pet.jpg" alt="PC+PET Alloy">

PC+PET Alloy

Good hardness, heat resistance, transparency and chemical resistance. Outstanding low-temperature impact strength.

Applications: Auto grilles, bumpers, ATM shells, generators, industrial parts, mobile shells.

< img src="nylon-asa.jpg" alt="Nylon+ASA Alloy">

Nylon+ABS/ASA Alloy

Weather resistance, thermal stability, natural matte surface. Density reduced up to 6% for cost saving.

Applications: Automotive interiors, sports equipment, power tools, ski boots, housings.

< img src="abs-pbt.jpg" alt="ABS+PBT Alloy">

ABS+PBT Alloy

Good flow, heat and cold resistance, high chemical resistance. Improved toughness and dimensional stability.

Glass fiber reinforced grade available for higher performance.

< img src="pmma-abs.jpg" alt="Acrylic+ABS Alloy">

Acrylic+ABS Alloy

Ultra-high gloss, high hardness, scratch resistance, spray-free, eco-friendly and beautiful surface.

Applications: Monitors, TVs, audio, home appliances, car electronics, chargers, printers.
