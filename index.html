公司简介 | Company Profile

广东中科聚能新材料科技有限公司是一家专注于PC合金改性、尼龙合金改性、ABS合金改性、PBT合金改性、ASA合金改性、亚克力合金改性的材料生产及研发企业。公司拥有完整科学的质量管理体系，具备现代化研发生产基地及检测中心，致力于为汽车、电子、家电等行业提供稳定、高性能的工程塑料解决方案。

Guangdong Zhongke Juneng New Materials Technology Co., Ltd. is a professional enterprise dedicated to the R&D and production of modified alloy materials, including PC alloy, PA alloy, ABS alloy, PBT alloy, ASA alloy and PMMA alloy. With a complete and scientific quality management system, modern R&D and production bases and testing centers, we are committed to providing stable and high-performance engineering plastic solutions for automotive, electronics, home appliances and other industries.

PC+PBT合金 | PC+PBT Alloy

既保持PBT的耐化学性、耐高温、易成型，又兼备PC的韧性、尺寸稳定性、耐候性与抗化学辅助性。对汽油、润滑油、冷却剂、植物油脂、醇类等耐应力开裂性优异，低温冲击强度高、光泽好。

Combines PBT's chemical resistance, high temperature resistance and easy molding with PC's toughness, dimensional stability, weather resistance and chemical auxiliary resistance. Excellent stress cracking resistance to gasoline, lubricants, coolants, vegetable oils, alcohols, etc., with high low-temperature impact strength and good gloss.

应用 | Applications：汽车散热格栅、保险杠、取款机外壳、发电机外壳、注塑机面板、路由器、天线、工业接头
