PA/ABS 合金 | PA/ABS Alloy (Nylon/ABS)

中文：耐候、热稳定、抗化学；耐汽油、润滑剂、冷却剂、油脂、醇类，耐应力开裂；尺寸稳定性优于尼龙，表面哑光，减震，密度降低约6%，降本。

English：Weather resistance, thermal stability, chemical resistance; resistant to gasoline, lubricants, coolants, oils, alcohols; better dimensional stability than nylon, matte surface, shock absorption, density reduced by ~6%, cost saving.

应用：汽车内饰（仪表板、中央控制面板）、运动器材、摩托/滑雪靴鞋跟、割草机外壳、电动工具外壳

Applications：Automotive interiors (dashboard, central control panel), sports equipment, motorcycle/ski boot heels, lawnmower housings, power tool casings
