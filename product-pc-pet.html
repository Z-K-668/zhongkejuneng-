PC/PET 合金 | PC/PET Alloy

中文：良好硬度、耐热、透明、热稳定、抗化学；耐汽油、润滑剂、冷却剂、油脂、醇类，耐应力开裂，低温冲击强、高光泽。

English：Good hardness, heat resistance, transparency, thermal stability, chemical resistance; resistant to gasoline, lubricants, coolants, oils, alcohols; excellent low-temperature impact, high gloss.

应用：汽车散热格栅、保险杠、取款机外壳、发电机外壳、注塑机面板、路由器、天线、工业鞋头、丝印、手机外壳

Applications：Automotive grilles, bumpers, ATM housings, generator casings, injection molding machine panels, routers, antennas, industrial shoe tips, screen printing, mobile phone housings
